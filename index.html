bitHaven

Goals

My vision for this website is to be a friendly forum for computer enthusiasts, with opensource resources for Cyber Security, game dev, web dev and more.
I feel current social media and message boards have lost their unique feel.
I'm hoping to finish out this website and host it locally on my own private network. That way I can develop my backend skills without having to pay for a domain or go through the risks of hosting a public domain.
I haven't been logging my progress, but I'll start doing that going forward to show my progress as I continue my journey.
